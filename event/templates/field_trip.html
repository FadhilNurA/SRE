FIELD TRIP

What is Field Trip?
The Field Trip is an event before the Grand Seminar, where registrants will visit a renewable energy office/power plant. During the visit, participants will tour the company's facilities, interact with industry professionals, and learn about innovative renewable energy sources. Experts will share insights on clean energy production, environmental impact, and career opportunities in the field. The event will prioritize finalists of the competitions and SRE UI members.

Objectives

  1. Providing participants with new insights and experiences related to the renewable energy sector and its applications in the industrial world.
  2. Gaining new knowledge about the production process flow and the management of renewable energy.
  3. Understanding industry profiles and the key preparations needed to become an ideal worker in the green jobs sector.

Expected Outcomes

  1. Increase participants' understanding of the renewable energy sector
  2. Gain direct experience in renewable energy sector jobs
  3. Foster partnerships between SRE UI and PLN for potential future collaborations

Date and Time

Friday, June 13, 2025

10:00 AM - 4:00 PM WIB

Location:

Saguling Hydroelectric Power Plant (PLTA Saguling), Clearneng Cipanas, Rajamandala Kulon, Cipalat District, West Bandung Regency, West Java 40554, Indonesia
