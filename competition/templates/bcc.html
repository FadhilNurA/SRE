Business Case Competition (BCC)

The Business Case Competition (BCC) by RES 2025 offers an exciting platform for students to tackle real-world sustainability challenges by developing creative, impactful, and feasible business strategies that promote renewable energy adoption.

About the Competition

BCC 2025 invites diploma (D3, D4) and undergraduate (S1) students from across Indonesia to present innovative business solutions in the sustainability sector. Participants are expected to design business models and strategies that enable industries to make a green transition and adopt renewable energy practices.
