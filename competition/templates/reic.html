Renewable Energy
Innovation Competition

The Renewable Energy Innovation Competition (REIC) is a premier competition organized under the Reenergize Summit by the Society of Renewable Energy Universitas Indonesia. REIC is crafted to explore the frontiers of innovation in the renewable energy sector, providing a platform for undergraduate students—both national and international—to unleash their creativity, strategic thinking, and technical prowess. In an era where innovation is crucial to accelerating the global energy transition, REIC empowers future changemakers to turn groundbreaking ideas into actionable, real-world solutions. Participants will tackle pressing challenges in renewable energy through immersive case studies and innovation challenges that mirror the complexities faced by today's energy innovators, startups, and sustainability leaders.
