ReEnergize Summit
Policy Case Competition

The RES 2025 Case Competition (PCC) invites passionate and forward-thinking students to tackle pressing energy policy issues in the DKI Jakarta region and surrounding areas. This competition challenges participants to develop innovative policy recommendations through real-world analysis and strategic thinking. The final presentations will be evaluated by a distinguished panel of policymakers and key stakeholders in the energy sector. PCC is a team-based competition (2-3 members per team) designed to enhance participants' policy analysis, critical thinking, and strategic decision-making skills. By engaging with real-world energy challenges, participants will gain valuable insights and contribute to sustainable energy policy development in Indonesia.
