ReEnergize Summit 2025 by SRE UI is the biggest annual event organized by SRE UI, invites the public to participate in an engaging lineup of activities centered on renewable energy. With support from renewable energy experts, SRE UI ReEnergize Summit to drive meaningful change in Indonesia’s energy transition by promoting energy diversification, community resilience, and sustainable careers in the green sector.
