SRE UI is a student chapter focused on nurturing future renewable energy leaders through learning, projects, and collaboration for UI students, in alignment with all stakeholders.

Our Vision

SRE UI as a sustainable organization with focus on renewable energy that nurtures member growth, enables impactful collaborations, and advances positive influences for continuous change.

Our Mission

1. Nurturing future leaders in renewable energy in their personal and professional development journey.

2. Aligning members purpose to learn and engage in organizational activities.

3. Empowering the youth to be aware and involved in renewable energy development movement.

4. Promoting supportive and constructive environment to drive progress among communities and stakeholders.

5. Elevating positive impact through programs and collaborations that benefit both our members and the broader society.

Our Core Value

Engage

Elevate

ReEnergize Summit 2025 by SRE UI is the biggest annual event organized by SRE UI, invites the public to participate in an engaging lineup of activities centered on renewable energy. The events within ReEnergize Summit 2025 includes Case Competition, Policy Case Competition, Renewable Energy Innovation Competition, Policy Workshop, Field Trip, and Pentahelix Talks all designed to facilitate the growth of knowledge within our local community. With support from renewable energy experts, SRE UI ReEnergize Summit to drive meaningful change in Indonesia's energy transition by promoting energy diversification, community resilience, and sustainable careers in the green sector.
